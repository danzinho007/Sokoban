Let's Logic Collection ID: 44
Title: SokHard
Author: Lee J Haywood
Description: The SokHard collection of Sokoban puzzle designs were generated by a program that used random numbers and Brian Damgaard's YASGen program to rapidly 'evolve' them, using the least number of pushes required to solve a design as its fitness value. Most of these designs feature a single target/goal area.
