Let's Logic Collection ID: 47
Title: Choriban
Author: Marti Homs Caussa
Description: This collection name derives from 'choripan', an spanish word that means 'sandwich of sausage'. This collection includes two subsets called 'chorimalism' (7) and 'susi' (10). The 'chorimalism' subset (that is, minimal levels for Choriban collection) is composed by levels of 2 boxes, and the subcollection called 'susi' is formed by levels of small dimensions, experimenting with different number of boxes, from 2 to 7. The rest of the levels have a small size, normally with 3 boxes. Finally, the last two levels are based in David W. Skinner's 'Microban 60', that I have called 'stack-machines'.
