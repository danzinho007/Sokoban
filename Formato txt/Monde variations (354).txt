Title: Monde variations
Author: Jordi Dom√©nech
Description: This collection brings together 95 \"variations\" of the puzzles of Ghislain \r\nMartin, most of them belonging to his fantastic collection \"Monde\". Some of \r\nthese \"variations\" are very difficult. I hesitated if include them in this \r\nselection, because all that is gained in difficulty, is lost in fun \r\n(especially difficult are the \"chessboards\" #31 and #42). Ghislain Martin\r\npuzzles are very clever and very fun, however, these \"variations\" are at risk\r\nof being difficult and boring. I recommend this: If you can\'t solve a \r\npuzzle, leave it for last, and try to solve the following. Enjoy! - J.D.\r\n\r\nThese levels are copyright (c) by Ghislain Martin and Jordi Domenech and may \r\nbe freely distributed for non-commercial use provided they remain unchanged, \r\ncredited with the author\'s name, and provided the author is notified.
