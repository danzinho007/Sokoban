Let's Logic Collection ID: 906
Title: Storage Rabbit
Author: mkiermaier
Description: Many of the Thinking Rabbit levels have a single-entrance storage area which can be solved independently of the full level. While some storage areas are obvious to solve, others require some planning. The main purpose of this collection is find and document optimal solutions, which can then be extended to good solutions of the full original level.
