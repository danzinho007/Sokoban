Let's Logic Collection ID: 664
Title: Compact Chaos 01
Author: Razorflame
Description: This pack contains the same kind of levels that can be found in Chaotic Catalysts, only they will be made in the same vein as my Compact Catalyst level sets.

These kinds of puzzles always seem to be very interesting to optimize as there always seems to be solutions with fewer moves and pushes found pretty often.

That being said, these are all hand-made and are made with the difficulty and fascination in mind.

Enjoy!
