Let's Logic Collection ID: 13
Title: Sasquatch 01
Author: David W. Skinner
Description: Sasquatch (50 puzzles, released January, 1999) This is my first set. Scott Lindhurst describes them as generally of just the right difficulty to be fun: not too easy, but (usually) not so hard that you get frustrated.

It is Not allowed to make this collection a part of any payable Sokoban program, such as Bjorn Kallmark's sokoban etc. You can make it a part of any freeware programs though.
