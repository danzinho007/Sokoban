Let's Logic Collection ID: 508
Title: Alberto Garcia remixed
Author: Jordi Doménech
Description: In a few months, between June and December 2002, Alberto Garcia published\r\n1000 puzzles, distributed in several collections. Alberto was a student\r\nof 18 years old. All of puzzles are very simple and easy, but there are \r\nsome good ideas. I\'ve remodeled some puzzles, and I have ordered according \r\nto increasing difficulty, although none of them exceeds the medium difficulty,\r\ntypical of the Thinking Rabbit puzzles. I am sure that in this \"remixed\" set,\r\nSokoban fans will discover a new Alberto Garcia. - J.D.\r\n\r\n\r\nThese levels are copyright (c) by Alberto Garcia and Jordi Doménech and may\r\nbe freely distributed for non-commercial use provided they remain unchanged,\r\ncredited with the author\'s name, and provided the author is notified.\r\n\r\nThis collection was downloaded from:\r\n\"Sokoban. The beautiful world of remodels\"\r\nhttp://sokoban-jd.blogspot.com/
